🌪️ Disaster Safety Fun!

Learn what to do with fun characters and animations!

🌊

Floods

Before 🌧️

  • Move to high ground ⛰️
  • Call 112/108/100 📞
  • Stay upstairs 🏠
  • Listen to warnings 👂

After ☀️

  • Don't touch wires ⚡
  • Avoid flood water 🚫
  • Use sticks/tires for support 🛞
  • Stay calm 😌
🌍

Earthquakes

During 🌍

  • Drop → Cover → Don't move 🛡️
  • Stay under sturdy furniture 🪑
  • Hold on until shaking stops 🤝

After ✅

  • Stay away from buildings 🏢
  • Check for injuries 🩹
  • Use medicines if needed 💊
  • Emergency: 1092 🚨
☀️

Droughts

Prevention 🌱

  • Conserve water 💧
  • Store emergency water 🗄️
  • Plant drought-resistant crops 🌾
  • Monitor weather reports 📊

During ☀️

  • Limit water usage 🚰
  • Use water-saving techniques 💡
  • Support community efforts 🤝
  • Stay informed 📰
🌀

Cyclones

During 🌪️

  • Stay inside 🏠
  • Avoid windows 🪟
  • Follow adults' instructions 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦
  • Emergency: 1070 🚨

SAFE Mnemonic 🧠

Stay inside 🏠
Avoid windows 🪟
Follow adults 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦
Emergency 1070 🚨
